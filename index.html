Kenya

Kenya is a country in East Africa with a coastline on the Indian Ocean. It features savannahs, lakelands, the dramatic Great Rift Valley, and mountain highlands. Kenya is home to incredible wildlife such as lions, elephants, and rhinos. The capital city is Nairobi.
