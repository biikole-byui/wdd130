About Kenya

Kenya is a country in East Africa with a coastline on the Indian Ocean. It features savannahs, lakelands, the dramatic Great Rift Valley, and mountain highlands. It is also home to incredible wildlife like lions, elephants, and rhinos. Kenya's capital city is Nairobi.
